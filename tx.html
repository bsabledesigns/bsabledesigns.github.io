TX

The project

TX is a company that links landlords with potential renters as well as renters with roommates through their app. In large cities with a shortage of places to rent, it’s difficult for the landlords, the renters and the roommates to find each other efficiently, and TX aims to change that.

The challenge

TX wanted to create an easy to use app based on the Tinder’s idea of swiping. In addition to creating an easy to use interface, they asked me to  figure out a way to incorporate different profile options without overwhelming users.

My Role

UX, Research & Product Development

Getting to a solution

My first step was to do research on the current products on the market along with Tinder. I worked with the stakeholder to brainstorm about how the product would work and create a features list. Users personas were created to keep users in mind when making the user flow and building wireframes and prototypes. The prototype was made in InVision, so users can demo the app to find potential problems in the usability and provide feedback.

Brainstorming & Features

I brainstormed with the client to determine what features were needed, created a features list, and ordered features by phases of app. 

 

User personas

I researched types of users through the major competitors, the largest one being craiglist.com and designed four user personas. The users personas helped me narrow down which features were needed for each kind of user.

User flow

I created a user flow to think about how the user could navigate the app easily.

Prototyping: Sketch & InVision

I used Sketch to build out screens which were incorporated in InVision to create the first prototype. I reiterated on the prototype as potential problems were found through user testing.
