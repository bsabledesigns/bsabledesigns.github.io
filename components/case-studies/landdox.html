Landdox

The Company

Landdox created a system that helps oil companies manage their land leases. Their goal is to help oil companies utilize the land they rent in a more efficient way.

The challenge

Landdox had pain points in a  page in which they needed users to upload information and display it on the same page. They requested I work with the current design within a small timeframe and not make major changes to the design. I like a good challenge, as they say constraint can bring creativity. I had to problem solve within the confines of their requirements.

My Role

UX & UI

Getting to a solution

My first step was to speak with the stakeholders about their users. As time was limited and I did not have access to the users to do testing, I gathered as much information as I could from the stakeholders. Users expressed frustration with the page where they upload documents (leases and tracks) and fill out forms based on the documents. Users were unable to navigate the page easily. 

To solve the problem I used background colors to differentiate the part of the page where people needed to fill in information and upload documents and the part of the page where information is displayed. I also placed ‘call to action’ buttons in intuitive places within the page and highlighted areas so the user would know what information is mandatory. I used tabs in the forms area so users could click between leases and tracks.
