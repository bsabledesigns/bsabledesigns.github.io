Dicks

The Company

Dicks is a comprehensive global provider of cloud-based Human Capital Management (HCM) solutions that unite HR, payroll, talent, time, tax and benefits administration, and a leader in business outsourcing services, analytics and compliance expertise. My team operates as an internal agency within Dicks, redesigning systems to optimize both internal and external user experience with products.

THE PROJECTS

Our team has successfully executed several projects aimed at improving employee benefits systems for clients and Dicks employees alike. My primary focus is redesigning systems to enhance user experience/satisfaction and augment client retention.

THE CHALLENGES

Several of the softwares that comprise the benefits systems have been obtained through acquisition and are, in some cases, complex, outdated and designed with little understanding of the end user. Our challenge is to work with the disparately developed systems—each possessing a distinct "look and feel"—to create a unified and cohesive experience for the user while identifying key pain points and streamlining their journey.

My Role

UX Design & Research

GETTING TO A SOLUTION

While the employee benefits projects share many similarities, each one requires a distinct solution that meets the unique needs of the users. However, in order to achieve these results, I follow the same deliberate process during each project: My first step is to meet with stakeholders to define the problem and ensure alignment in terms of our final objectives. From this, we are able to move forward into the discovery phase. During which, key issues/users are identified and we are able to begin to formulate solutions that address the client's particular pain points. As each client's challenge is different, I take the time to interview users to understand their individual needs as well as the business need. Once the solution is more clearly defined, I accompany the users through a testing process and receive their feedback. This can be an iterative process, however it ensures that the users needs are fully met and prevents the team from having to rebuild designs, optimizing project times. During the testing phase, I work collaboratively with development and POs to design and iterate based on feedback. This includes developing user flows and personas, brainstorming, sketching, prototyping, testing, designing and (re)iterating. This process allows me to uncover and address critical user pain points and improve their flow by creating careful and customized designs that result in user time savings, higher client retention, and friendlier products for the Dicks to sell to their clients. *Project clients and details can not be listed due to confidentiality agreements
